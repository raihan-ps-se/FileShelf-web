Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Overview

FileShelf is a macOS utility that acts as a temporary file clipboard.

FileShelf collects no personal data, sends nothing over the internet, and has no servers or accounts.

Data collection

FileShelf collects nothing. No analytics, no crash reports, no usage data, no personal information of any kind.

Network access

FileShelf makes no network requests. Your files and activity stay entirely on your device.

Local storage

FileShelf saves a list of file paths locally on your Mac so the shelf persists between launches. This data never leaves your device and is cleared when you remove files from the shelf or uninstall the app.

Permissions

FileShelf may request Accessibility access. This is optional and is only used to detect when you are dragging a file so the shelf can appear automatically. Denying this permission has no effect on any other feature.
