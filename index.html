Features

Everything you need.
Nothing you don't.

🪄
Auto-show on drag

Start dragging any file and hold for 1.5 seconds — FileShelf floats up automatically, no keyboard, no interruption.

hold drag · 1.5s
⌨️
Global shortcut

Toggle the shelf from any app, any space, any moment with ⌥Space. No accessibility permissions needed.

⌥Space
📋
Click to copy

Click any file on the shelf to copy it to your clipboard. Then ⌘V it into any app — no drag required.

click → ⌘V anywhere
🚀
Launches at login

Set FileShelf to start automatically when you log in — your shelf is ready before you even open your first app.

right-click menu bar icon
💾
Persists across relaunches

Files stay on the shelf until you remove them — even after quitting and reopening the app. Nothing gets lost.

🗜️
Zip & AirDrop built in

Compress selected files into a named ZIP (saved to /tmp) or AirDrop them to any nearby device — right from the shelf.

action bar

Privacy & permissions

Minimal footprint.
Zero trust required.

FileShelf asks for only what it needs — and tells you exactly why. You can deny everything; it still works.

⌨️
Accessibility Optional
Enables the auto-show on drag trigger. FileShelf prompts once on first launch — deny it and everything else still works perfectly.
🌐
Network access Never
No network requests — not on launch, not ever. Your files never leave your machine.
📁
File modification Never
Files are referenced only — never moved, copied, or modified. The shelf is a scratch space, not storage.
⚙️
Background processes Never
No launch daemons, no helper processes, no background activity beyond the app itself running in your menu bar.
